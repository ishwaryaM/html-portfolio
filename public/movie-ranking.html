Ajith has acted so well in this movie. He has showcased his acting potential in both protagonist and antagonist roles.

I became fan of ajith from the Billa movie. Whenever he acts in dual role, he is nailing it. So, i wish that ajith does more dual role movies.

Ajith exhibited a stylish acting skill in this movie. It's a treat to watch.
